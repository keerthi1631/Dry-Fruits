About Us

DRY FRUITS

Dry fruits, concentrated forms of fresh fruits with water removed, are nutrient-dense snacks packed with vitamins, minerals, fiber, and antioxidants, offering energy boosts, improved digestion, and better heart and bone health, making them a healthy, convenient, and long-lasting food choice, though best enjoyed in moderation due to their concentrated sugars and calories. Dry fruits are nature's concentrated gems, offering concentrated goodness in a convenient, shelf-stable package; by removing water, fruits like dates, figs, raisins, and apricots retain vital fiber, antioxidants, vitamins (A, K, E), and minerals (iron, magnesium).

Dry fruits are nutriet-dense, energy-boosting snacks packed with healthy fats, fiber, and vitamins, including almonds, cashews, walnuts, raisins, figs, and pistachios. They are ideal for improving digestion, boosting immunity, and heart health. Commonly consumed daily, they can be added to desserts, salads, or eaten raw for a healthy snack.

Dry Fruit Varieties & Uses

  • Common Nuts: Almonds (Badam), Cashews (Kaju), Walnuts (Akhrot), Pistachios (Pista).

  • Dried Fruits: Raisins (Kishmish), Figs (Anjeer), Apricots (Jardalu), Dates (Khajoor), Prunes.

  • Healthy Snacks: Fox nuts (Makhana), Hazelnuts, Pine nuts (Chilgoza), Brazil nuts.
