DRY FRUITS

🥜Dry fruits are nutritious foods obtained by removing the water content from fresh fruits while retaining most of their essential nutrients. 🫘They are rich in vitamins, minerals, fiber, and healthy fats, making them an excellent addition to a balanced diet. 🍑Common dry fruits such as almonds, cashews, walnuts, raisins, dates, and pistachios help improve digestion, boost immunity, and provide instant energy. 🍇 Regular consumption of dry fruits supports heart health, strengthens bones, and enhances brain function. Because of their long shelf life and high 🟤 nutritional value,dry fruits are often included in daily meals, desserts, and snacks, especially for maintaining overall health and wellness.
